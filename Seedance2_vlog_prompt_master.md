# AI Vlog Prompt Master

A clean GitHub-ready markdown template for creating:

- character reference sheet prompts
- character description prompts
- vlog prompt generators
- Seedance / Sora-style video prompts
- reusable shot-based cinematic vlog structures

## 6) Example Prompt From the Screenshot

This is the style reference shown in the image.

```text
[Aesthetic] Cinematic vlog. Frozen tundra. Selfie handheld. Naturalistic cold overcast light.

[Storyline] A modern man time-travels to the Ice Age and vlogs in real time, reacting to the landscape, the extreme cold, and a woolly mammoth herd that passes dangerously close.

[Characters] Character in @Image 1, using modern London accent, well articulated, wildly underdressed for the conditions. Background: a woolly mammoth herd at distance. On the far horizon, primitive humans wrapped in thick animal fur pelts and crude leather bindings gathered around a fire.

[Environment] Vast frozen tundra under a pale overcast sky. Cracked glacier ice underfoot. Snowdrift ridges. Sparse dead trees glazed with ice. A mammoth herd moving across the middle distance. Distant orange glow of a campfire on the horizon. Constant blizzard wind.

[Action Sequence]
SHOT 1 (0:00-0:02) Selfie handheld wide. He holds camera at arm's length, breath steaming heavily. Spins slowly to show the frozen landscape behind him. Eyes wide with disbelief.
DIALOGUE: "I'm in the Ice Age. This is actually the Ice Age."

SHOT 2 (0:02-0:05) Selfie close, low angle into wind. He hunches against the blizzard, pulling hoodie tighter with one hand while holding camera with the other. Laughs nervously at being this underdressed.
DIALOGUE: "I am so cold. This is not a normal amount of cold."

SHOT 3 (0:05-0:08) Camera turned outward. He points lens away from himself toward the mammoth herd at medium distance, walking slowly. Steps closer, arm fully extended. DIALOGUE (offscreen whisper): "Look. Look at them. That is an actual mammoth herd."

SHOT 4 (0:08-0:10) Selfie tight. One mammoth passes close. Massive shaggy flank fills the background. He goes completely still. Mouth slightly open. DIALOGUE (barely a whisper): "Don't move. Don't move."

SHOT 5 (0:10-0:13) Selfie wide. He tracks the mammoth as it clears frame. Exhales loudly once it passes. Laughs in disbelief, hand over mouth. Turns back to camera. DIALOGUE: "Oh my god. That was right there. That was right there."

SHOT 6 (0:13-0:15) Selfie close. He turns to face the distant campfire glow on the horizon. Sees the fur-clad silhouettes gathered around it. Studies them for a beat. Then slowly turns back to the lens.
DIALOGUE: "Those are people. First people. I need to go see."

[Production Brief] Natural cold overcast light. Breath visible in every shot. Selfie arm in frame throughout. Handheld shake from wind and cold. Shallow depth of field, subject sharp, mammoths in soft focus. Cool grey-white color grade. No warm tones anywhere.

[Negative Prompt] No music, no voiceover, no text on screen, no transitions, no warm colour grade, no artificial snow VFX.
```

## 7) Recommended Workflow

Use this order for the cleanest result:

1. Upload reference images.
2. Generate the character description.
3. Generate the character sheet.
4. Write the vlog prompt.
5. Convert it into the final Seedance / video prompt.
